The Bronx is where hip hop, a particular culture and art form, first emerged in the 1970s. It started in urban Puerto Rican/Hispanic, Latino, Jamaican, and African American neighborhoods in some of the bigger American cities. Rapping, in which an individual or group chants or mouths phrases in a rhythm that rhymes, is a component of hip hop. It uses drum rhythms created by a drum machine. Hip-hop songs frequently feature lyrics that discuss urban living in large cities. Hip-hop music also incorporates pop music genres like reggae and disco. Hip hop and rap have become popular musical genres.


Hip hop is a culture that includes both music and clothing.
